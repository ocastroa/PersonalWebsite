I started college as a physics major but switched after taking an intro to computer science course. While I enjoyed reading about space and quantum theory, I enjoyed programming more. I was able to create anything from scratch! In my third year I became curious about mobile development, so I learned how to build basic Android apps by watching Youtube videos. When I had enough knowledge, I decided to develop my first Android app, Big O, which I published in the play store. From then on I was hooked! I worked on three more Android apps throughout college and four web applications, including this site. You can checkout out my projects here.

When I am not brainstorming ideas for a new project or working on a current project, I like to read, play guitar, listen to music, or watch Star Trek!
